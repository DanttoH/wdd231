Scope Mendoza - Site Plan

Site Name

Scope Mendoza
This name was chosen to represent a local chamber of commerce in Mendoza, Argentina. It emphasizes the chamber's scope to support and promote local businesses and community development.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a hub for local businesses and entrepreneurs in Mendoza. It provides a member directory, current events, a weather update, and a call-to-action to join the chamber. The goal is to foster business visibility, economic growth, and collaboration through digital outreach.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a directory of local businesses affiliated with the chamber?
  • How do I become a member of Scope Mendoza and what are the benefits?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #194759 (Dark Teal Blue) – used for headers, navigation background, and footers.

Accent Color: #F3A712 (Golden Yellow) – used for buttons, call-to-action sections, and highlights.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Roboto Slab', serif – used for all main headings.

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – used for paragraphs and smaller UI text.
