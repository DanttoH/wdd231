Site Plan: Discover Natural Mendoza

Site Name

Discover Natural Mendoza

Purpose

To promote natural destinations in Mendoza and encourage sustainable tourism through eco-friendly activities and outdoor experiences.

Scenarios

  • What natural reserves can I visit in Mendoza?
  • Where can I go hiking or birdwatching?
  • How can I book a tour or reserve entry to a national park?

Color Scheme & Typography

Colors: Natural green, earthy brown, clean white.

Typography: 'Open Sans' for body text, 'Merriweather' for headings.

Wireframes

Mobile and desktop wireframes are included as images.
